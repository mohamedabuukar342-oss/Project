❓ Help & Instructions

🚀 How to Navigate

  • Home: Overview of TransportPro services with quick booking links.
  • Gallery: View transportation images and statistics table.
  • Booking: Complete the form to book flights, trains, buses, or cars.
  • Help: You're here! Get all the instructions you need.
  • About: Project details and developer information.

📝 Booking Instructions

  • All fields with * are required.
  • Select your preferred transport type from the dropdown.
  • Enter your destination and travel date.
  • Choose your gender and any additional services.
  • Write any special requests in the text area.
  • Click Confirm Booking to complete your reservation.

📸 Gallery Guide

  • View 3 transportation images (airplane, train, bus).
  • Check the data table with transport statistics.
  • Hover over table rows to see the pseudo-class effect.
  • Click external links for more transportation resources.
